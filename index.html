Frequently asked

Quick answers.

How fast is "same-day"?
Most ASAP bookings are picked up within 90 minutes of confirmation. Schedule a window for arrivals as precise as a 30-minute slot.
What if my item gets damaged?
Every delivery is insured up to $25K. Claims are filed in-app and resolved within 5 business days, guaranteed.
Do you deliver outside Bakersfield?
Yes — we cover greater Kern County including Shafter, Wasco, Delano, and Tehachapi (scheduled). Long-haul quotes available on request.
Will the crew bring it inside and assemble?
Standard and Heavy Haul include in-room placement. Add assembly/disassembly at booking — most jobs are flat-fee add-ons starting at $25.
How are your drivers vetted?
All drivers are background-checked, trained on safe-lift technique, and carry commercial cargo insurance. Most are local, full-time team members.
